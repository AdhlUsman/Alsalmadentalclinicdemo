Brief History of the Clinic

Founded in 2010, Alsalama Dental Clinic has grown from a small local practice into a trusted name in dental care. Our journey began with one goal: to bring expert dental treatment to every family in a comfortable and affordable way. Over the years, we’ve treated thousands of patients and earned their trust with gentle, honest, and high-quality care.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission: To deliver compassionate, comprehensive dental care using modern technology in a patient-friendly environment.

Our Vision: To become the region’s most recommended dental clinic known for care, safety, and service excellence.
